AGENBIO App Development Resources

Welcome to the reference page for building your AI-powered biotech application. Below are links to resources and tools you can use, including AI models, frameworks, and bioinformatics tools for drug discovery, molecular modeling, and data analysis.

AI & Machine Learning Frameworks

  • Hugging Face - A popular platform for natural language processing (NLP) and other AI models, including pretrained transformers for research and production use.
  • PyTorch - A deep learning framework widely used for building AI models and training neural networks.

AI Research Papers and Resources

  • arXiv - Open-access repository for research papers in AI, machine learning, and other scientific fields. It includes papers related to both theory and application of AI.
  • IEEE Xplore - A digital library for IEEE journals, conferences, and standards, including cutting-edge research in AI and machine learning.
  • Papers with Code - A site that connects machine learning papers with their implementations, providing code links alongside academic articles to facilitate reproducibility.

Notable AI Papers:

  • Attention is All You Need (Transformer Model) - This paper by Vaswani et al. introduces the transformer architecture, revolutionizing the field of NLP.
  • MobileNetV3: Efficient Convolutional Neural Networks for Mobile Vision Applications - This paper by Howard et al. presents MobileNetV3, an efficient CNN model for mobile applications, focusing on optimizing performance while minimizing computational complexity.
  • BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers for Language Understanding - A significant paper on BERT, a transformer-based model that has set new records in NLP tasks.
  • AlphaFold: Using AI for Protein Folding - This paper from DeepMind explores the use of AI in solving protein folding problems, a breakthrough in the intersection of AI and biotechnology.

Bioinformatics and Chemoinformatics Tools

  • RDKit - A powerful library for cheminformatics, including tools for molecular manipulation, descriptor calculation, and similarity searches.
  • PubChem (PubChem Database) - A public chemical database providing information on the biological activities of small molecules. Useful for drug discovery and molecular searches.

Additional Resources for Bio Development

  • GenomeWeb - Provides news and resources for the latest advancements in genomics, bioinformatics, and biotechnology.
  • NCBI - The National Center for Biotechnology Information, offering a wealth of bioinformatics tools and datasets for biomedical research.
  • Bioinformatics Journal - A leading journal in bioinformatics and computational biology, offering research articles and insights on bio-computational methods.
  • EMBnet - A network offering bioinformatics tools and services for the global community of life sciences researchers.

Bio-AI Integration and Drug Discovery Resources

  • DeepMind's AI for Drug Discovery - Exploring how AI is revolutionizing drug discovery, including advancements in protein folding with AlphaFold.
  • bioRxiv - Preprint server for the latest research in biology, genomics, and bioinformatics.
  • AI in Drug Discovery: A Survey - Article discussing the impact of AI on drug discovery processes, including machine learning and deep learning methods.
